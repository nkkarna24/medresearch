What We Deliver

Built on Cochrane & PRISMA Standards

Every systematic review we produce is grounded in full transparency at each stage. Our team of 20+ experienced researchers does the work manually — protocol, search, screening, extraction, and synthesis — with no automation or AI shortcuts.

📑

Protocol Registration

We draft and register your review protocol on PROSPERO or Open Science Framework before any screening begins, ensuring methodological accountability.

🔍

Comprehensive Search

Boolean search strategies across PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, Embase, Cochrane Library, and grey literature — fully documented and transparent.

👥

Dual Screening

Title/abstract and full-text screening performed independently by two reviewers, with conflict resolution and a documented PRISMA flow diagram.

📊

Data Extraction

Pilot-tested extraction forms capture study characteristics and effect sizes. All data are double-extracted to minimise error and bias.

⚠️

Risk of Bias

Study-level quality assessed with Cochrane RoB 2 (RCTs), ROBINS-I (non-randomised), QUADAS-2 (diagnostic), or a tool tailored to your design.

📈

PRISMA Flow Diagram

Publication-ready PRISMA 2020 flow diagram documenting every record from identification to inclusion, with reasons for exclusion.

🏆

GRADE Assessment

Certainty of evidence evaluated with the GRADE framework across all outcomes, delivered as Summary of Findings (SoF) tables.

🔄

Meta-Analysis Integration

Statistical pooling with random/fixed-effects models, heterogeneity (I²), subgroup and sensitivity analyses, funnel plots, and forest plots.

The Process

Our 5-Step Process

A transparent, fully documented workflow — from research question to publishable manuscript. Every step is manual, auditable, and reproducible.

📑

Protocol

Formulate the research question, define eligibility criteria, and register the protocol on PROSPERO or OSF.

🔍

Search

Develop and execute Boolean search strategies across 5+ databases plus grey literature sources.

👥

Screening

Dual independent title/abstract and full-text screening with conflict resolution by a third reviewer.

📊

Extraction

Double data extraction, risk of bias assessment, and GRADE certainty evaluation across outcomes.

📝

Report

PRISMA flow diagram, narrative synthesis, meta-analysis, and a manuscript-ready output.
