Whether you have raw extracted data or just a research question, our team of 20+ experienced researchers delivers a publication-ready meta-analysis with full statistical rigor and reproducible workflows.

💡 Don't have a research question yet? No problem. Our researchers will help you identify a focused, answerable question based on your field of interest — included in every plan at no additional cost.

📊

Frequentist & Bayesian Models

Fixed-effects, random-effects (DerSimonian-Laird, REML), and Bayesian hierarchical models with informative priors. We select the best approach for your data and research question.

🧩

Heterogeneity Exploration

Quantified via I², Q-statistic, Tau², and prediction intervals. We explore sources of heterogeneity through meta-regression and subgroup analysis, not just report it.

🔄

Subgroup Analysis

Pre-specified and post-hoc subgroup analyses to identify effect modifiers. Categorical and continuous moderators examined with mixed-effects models and meta-regression.

🔬

Sensitivity Analysis

Leave-one-out, influence diagnostics, cumulative meta-analysis, and model comparison. Every result is stress-tested to ensure robustness before final reporting.

🔍

Publication Bias Assessment

Funnel plots, Egger's test, Begg's test, trim-and-fill, and selection models. We quantify and adjust for small-study effects and reporting biases systematically.

📈

Forest & Funnel Plots

Publication-ready forest plots with custom annotations, and contour-enhanced funnel plots. All figures formatted for journal submission in TIFF/PDF/SVG.

⭐

GRADE Integration

We assess and report the certainty of evidence using GRADE (Grading of Recommendations, Assessment, Development, and Evaluations) for every outcome.

💬

Results Interpretation

Plain-language interpretation of statistical findings alongside rigorous technical reporting. We write the discussion, limitations, and implications in publication-ready prose.

The Process

From Research Question to Published Meta-Analysis

A systematic 5-step workflow designed for methodological rigor, reproducibility, and publication success.

📑

Protocol

We define the research question, PICO framework, search strategy, eligibility criteria, and analysis plan. Optionally registered in PROSPERO or OSF.

🔍

Data Extraction

Systematic literature search, dual screening, and structured data extraction. Effect sizes, variances, moderators, and risk-of-bias assessment. Full PRISMA flow diagram.

📊

Statistical Analysis

Frequentist or Bayesian meta-analysis with heterogeneity quantification, subgroup and sensitivity analyses, meta-regression, publication bias testing, and NMA where appropriate.

🧠

Interpretation

Results interpreted in clinical and methodological context. GRADE certainty assessment, forest and funnel plots, summary tables, and clear narrative synthesis.

📄

Report

Full manuscript written in your target journal's format (PRISMA 2020 compliant). Abstract, main text, figures, tables, references, and supplementary materials included.
