Review Types

Evidence Synthesis Tailored to Your Needs

From broad narrative overviews to methodologically rigorous systematic reviews, our team delivers evidence syntheses that meet academic and publication standards.

📜

Narrative Review

A broad, descriptive overview of the literature on a topic. Ideal for introductions, textbook chapters, or when a comprehensive but non-systematic summary is needed.

🔍

Systematic Literature Review

Methodical, reproducible search following PRISMA guidelines. Includes database search strategy, dual screening, quality assessment, and evidence synthesis with clear inclusion criteria.

⚡

Rapid Review

A streamlined systematic review delivered in weeks, not months. Uses abbreviated methodology to produce timely evidence summaries for urgent decision-making or policy briefs.

📊

Evidence Synthesis

Structured aggregation of findings across multiple studies. Includes summary tables, forest plots (where appropriate), and GRADE-based assessment of evidence quality.

🔄

Living Reviews

Continuously updated reviews that incorporate new evidence as it emerges. Ideal for fast-moving fields where the evidence base evolves rapidly over time.

🎯

Thematic Analysis

Identification and synthesis of key themes, concepts, and debates across the literature. Highlights trends, gaps, contradictions, and emerging directions in the field.

📋

Citation Management

Full reference management in your preferred format (APA, AMA, Vancouver, Nature, Harvard, etc.). All citations verified against original sources for accuracy.

🖼️

Visual Summaries

PRISMA flow diagrams, evidence gap maps, concept maps, summary-of-findings tables, and graphical abstracts to communicate your review findings clearly.

The Process

From Question to Completed Review

A structured 4-stage methodology designed for rigor, reproducibility, and timely delivery.

🎯

Scope & Plan

We define your research question, determine the appropriate review type, develop the search strategy, and establish inclusion/exclusion criteria.

🔍

Search & Screen

Systematic database searches (PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, Embase, etc.). Dual screening of titles/abstracts and full texts against eligibility criteria.

📝

Synthesize & Analyze

Data extraction, quality appraisal, thematic or quantitative synthesis, and identification of key findings, gaps, and implications.

🚀

Report & Deliver

Final review manuscript with structured sections, summary tables, visual aids, and full reference list — formatted to your target journal or use case.
