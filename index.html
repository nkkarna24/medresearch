How It Works

A Proven, Transparent Process

Every project follows a structured pipeline to ensure quality, reproducibility, and timely delivery.

Step 01
Scoping & Proposal
We discuss your research question, objectives, timeline, and deliverables. You receive a detailed proposal with fixed pricing.
Step 02
Protocol & Registration
We draft a protocol (PROSPERO, OSF, or your preferred registry), define eligibility criteria, search strategy, and analysis plan.
Step 03
Search & Screening
Comprehensive database searches (PubMed, Embase, Cochrane, Scopus, Web of Science) with dual independent screening in Covidence/Rayyan.
Step 04
Data Extraction & Analysis
Structured data extraction, risk of bias assessment, and statistical analysis in RevMan, R, Stata, or JAGS — fully reproducible.
Step 05
Writing & Revision
Manuscript drafting with unlimited revision rounds. PRISMA flow diagrams, forest plots, and tables included.
Step 06
Submission Support
Journal formatting, cover letter, responses to reviewers, and post-acceptance proofing — we stay with you through publication.
