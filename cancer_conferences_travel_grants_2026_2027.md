### Notes
- Several ESMO family meetings (TAT, Breast, Sarcoma/Rare, ELCC, GI, MAP, AI/Digital, IO, Asia, GU Asia 2027) all share the **ESMO Travel Grant** programme: free registration + funds toward travel/accommodation, primarily for presenters from low- and middle-income countries and ESMO members under 40 in training. Application is integrated into abstract submission.
- AACR meetings share the **Scholar-in-Training / NextGen Stars / Women in Cancer Research / Global Scholar** travel awards covering registration, economy airfare (domestic up to ~$850, international up to ~$1,700), and lodging up to a ~$4,000 ceiling.
- EACR offers **Travel Grants** (free registration + travel/accommodation) for its focused conferences, supported by Worldwide Cancer Research.
- All dates and deadlines above were verified against official society/conference pages or authoritative oncology calendars (OncoDaily, Clocate, society sites). Where a 2026 date was not yet officially posted (e.g., some 2027 meetings), the typical month is noted and marked TBA where appropriate.
