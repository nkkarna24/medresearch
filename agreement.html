Service Agreement & Payment

Please review the project summary and terms below, then accept to proceed to your secure payment link.

1. Scope of Services

The service described above (the "Project") will be performed by medresearch.me ("Provider") in accordance with the specifications agreed between the Provider and the client ("Client"). Any work beyond the stated scope may require a separate agreement and additional fees.

2. Payment

By proceeding to payment, the Client agrees to pay for this Project via the unique payment link assigned to it. Payment is structured as chosen by the Provider for this Project:

Full payment: 100% of the total amount is paid upfront before work begins.

Split payment (50 / 50): 50% of the total is paid upfront to commence the Project; the remaining 50% is paid after the Project is completed. Project files and the Right Transfer Agreement are released to the Client only once the full amount has been paid.

Prices are exclusive of any taxes unless stated otherwise.

3. Revisions & Deliverables

Where a plan includes revisions, these are limited to the scope defined in the service description. Turnaround times are estimates and begin after all required materials and information are received from the Client.

4. Confidentiality

All Client data, manuscripts, and identifiers shared with the Provider will be treated as confidential and used solely for the fulfillment of the Project, in line with our Privacy Policy.

5. Right Transfer Agreement

Upon receipt of full payment for this Project (the single full payment, or both halves of a split plan), medresearch.me transfers to the Client all rights, title, and interest in the final deliverables. The deliverables become the Client's property and may be used, published, submitted, modified, or transferred by the Client without further restriction. medresearch.me retains no rights to republish Client-specific content without permission. This transfer takes legal effect automatically once full payment is recorded.

6. Cancellation & Refunds

Refund eligibility is assessed on a case-by-case basis and communicated by the Provider. For split plans, the deposit (first 50%) is non-refundable once work has commenced. Work delivered is non-refundable.

7. Acceptance

By accepting this agreement, the Client confirms the Project details are correct and authorizes payment through the assigned link.

You will be redirected to your unique payment link after accepting. This action is recorded for your records.
